RECOMMENDATIONS

OILY DRY COMBINATION NORMAL

OILY SKIN

Key Traits: Shiny, enlarged pores, prone to acne/breakouts

Recommended Routine:

  • Cleanser: Gentle foaming or gel cleanser (oil-control, non-comedogenic)
  • Moisturizer: Lightweight, oil-free or gel-based moisturizer
  • Sunscreen: Matte-finish SPF, oil-free formula
  • Weekly Care: Clay mask once or twice a week to reduce excess oil

Best Ingredients: Salicylic acid, niacinamide, tea tree, green tea

Avoid: Heavy creams and overly harsh scrubs (they can cause more oil production)

DRY SKIN

Key Traits: Feels tight, rough, or flaky, sometimes dull-looking

Recommended Routine:

  • Cleanser: Hydrating cream or milk cleanser (avoid foaming ones)
  • Moisturizer: Rich, nourishing moisturizer with ceramides or shea butter
  • Sunscreen: Hydrating SPF with moisturizing base
  • Weekly Care: Gentle exfoliation (enzyme-based or mild AHA) + hydrating mask

Best Ingredients: Hyaluronic acid, glycerin, ceramides, squalane

Avoid: Alcohol-based toners, strong foaming cleansers

COMBINATION SKIN

Key Traits: Balanced, smooth, rarely sensitive, minimal issues

Recommended Routine:

  • Cleanser: Gentle foaming or cream cleanser
  • Moisturizer: Lightweight hydrating moisturizer
  • Sunscreen: Broad-spectrum SPF for daily protection
  • Weekly Care: Gentle exfoliation once a week to maintain skin health

Best Ingredients: Vitamin C, hyaluronic acid, peptides

Avoid: Over-cleansing or using too many harsh actives (can disrupt balance)

NORMAL SKIN

Key Traits: Oily in the T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) but dry/normal on cheeks

Recommended Routine:

  • Cleanser: Gentle gel cleanser to balance oil and dryness
  • Moisturizer: Lightweight lotion or gel that hydrates without heaviness

  • Weekly Care: Multi-masking (clay mask on T-zone, hydrating mask on cheeks)

Best Ingredients: Niacinamide, green tea, hyaluronic acid

Avoid: Over-drying acne treatments all over the face
